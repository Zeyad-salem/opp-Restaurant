About our restaurant

OPP Restaurant is an American fast food company, founded in 1940 as a restaurant operated by Richard and Maurice , in San Bernardino, California, United States. They rechristened their business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 1953 at a location in Phoenix, Arizona. In 1955
